Coauthorship network of researchers studying science communication

Coauthorship network for a set of research papers in the fields of science communication. Nodes represent authors, and weighted links represent collaborations between authors on research papers. The network was clustered using Infomap. The colors of the nodes are assigned based on the top level of the hierarichal clustering. The size of the nodes represent the flow-based rank of each node in this network. The connected component with ten nodes or more are shown. Click and drag the nodes to move them around.
